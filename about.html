About Us

Hands free hosting made simple.

Welcome to WeDoProperty, your dedicated partner in full-service short-term rental management. We specialize in taking the hassle out of hosting by providing property owners with a comprehensive, hands-off solution — so you can enjoy the benefits of rental income without the day-to-day stress. From initial setup to ongoing operations, our expert team manages every aspect of your property with precision and care. We handle listings across top booking platforms, develop dynamic pricing strategies to maximize your returns, and ensure every guest enjoys a five-star experience through clear communication and prompt support. Our services go far beyond booking. We maintain meticulous financial records, conduct thorough property inspections before and after every stay, and coordinate professional cleaning and maintenance to keep your home in perfect condition. We document and report any damages, manage claims through platform insurance, and oversee repairs or replacements as needed — protecting your investment at every step. Your property’s safety and reputation are our priority. With detailed guest screening, personalized onboarding, and attentive guest relations, we create a seamless experience that builds positive reviews and repeat bookings. At WeDoProperty, we combine technology with a human touch to deliver reliable, high-quality service you can trust. Whether you own one home or several, we're here to make property ownership easier, more profitable, and fully supported — every step of the way.

Our Story

Built in Cape Town. Backed by Trust. Driven by Growth. WeDoProperty was founded in Cape Town by Safeera Neacsu, a passionate local property investor and sourcing specialist with a vision: to create a management service that offers true peace of mind to property owners — grounded in safety, transparency, and exceptional service. What began with just a few carefully curated properties soon evolved into a trusted solution for owners seeking a hands-free, fully managed rental experience. From day one, Safeera’s commitment was clear — to protect every client’s investment with the same care and attention as her own. By 2025, the company’s impact caught the attention of European joint venture partners, who saw the potential for rapid growth. With an infusion of international capital, WeDoProperty began scaling operations, expanding its team, and investing in top-tier systems, technology, and service delivery. Today, WeDoProperty is focused on expansion and excellence — building a strong team, preparing for further capital inflow, and bringing in new properties under management. Yet, at its core, the mission remains the same: To provide property owners with a safe, streamlined, and highly professional management service that delivers superior guest experiences and consistent returns. As we grow, we continue to uphold the values we were founded on — reliability, service quality, and protecting our clients’ peace of mind.
